The 5 W's

Who

Human trafficking can happen to anyone of any age but tends to happen to people aging from 14- 24 more than other ages. Age is not a factor that is heavily weighed for human trafficking. Any person at the wrong time could be victimized.

What

Human trafficking is about people being taken against their will and being forced to do illegal or maybe illicit activities. Human trafficking violates Section 2(fundamental freedom) of the charter of rights.

When

Human trafficking is an issue that is growing rapidly in Canada. There is no specific time for when human trafficking occurs. It can happen at any given time but usually tends to happen at nighttime

Where

Human trafficking can happen at any place. A person could be taken against their will from a park or the side of the street or a dark alleyway. The venue could also span to cities where human traffickers have specific routes which they take when trafficking people.

Why

Human trafficking can occur for a number of reasons, some being: Money, labour, sexual reasons, revenge etc.
