Taking Action

Which level of government does human trafficking concern?

Human trafficking concerns every single level of government. Federal government should stop international trafficking. Provinces should stop national trafficking and municipalities should implement security precautions in the city or town.

How to approach the government official

The government official could be reached through protests, emails, at scheduled meetings, calling on a phone, letters etc

What are some action a person can take as an individual?

Speaking in public places, giving speeches, Peaceful protests, Petitions and websites are all great ways to address this issue to a large number of people. Nowadays a youtube channel or blog would also help vastly in reaching audiences across the country and even the globe.
