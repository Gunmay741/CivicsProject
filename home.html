Human Trafficking

This is a website made to spread awareness about Human traficking. It is a horrible crime that infringes upon a person's fundamental freedoms which are listed in Section 2 of the charter of rights. To make this topic a little less depressing i have coded in a simple game in which you play as a baby reporting a Human Trafficker.
